## Finding Organizations to Support

### Discovery Tools

#### Browse by Category
- **Education**: Schools, scholarships, literacy programs
- **Health**: Medical research, healthcare access, mental health
- **Environment**: Conservation, climate action, sustainability
- **Social Services**: Food security, housing, community support
- **Emergency Relief**: Disaster response, humanitarian aid

#### Search and Filter Options
- **Geographic location**: Local, national, or international
- **Organization size**: Large NGOs or grassroots organizations
- **Verification status**: Fully verified organizations only
- **Impact metrics**: Organizations with proven results
- **Tax-deductible**: Filter for tax-eligible donations

### Evaluating Organizations

#### Verification Checklist
- ✅ **Verified badge** - Platform-verified credentials
- ✅ **Tax status** - Valid charitable registration
- ✅ **Financial transparency** - Open financial reporting
- ✅ **Impact reporting** - Measurable outcomes
- ✅ **Recent activity** - Active and responsive

#### Due Diligence Questions
- What specific problems does this organization solve?
- How does the organization measure and report impact?
- What percentage of donations goes directly to programs?
- Are there any recent scandals or concerns?
- Do they have endorsements from trusted sources?

## Making Donations

### Choosing Your Donation Method

#### One-Time Donations
- **Immediate impact** for urgent needs
- **Flexibility** to adjust amounts
- **Testing organizations** with smaller gifts
- **Special occasions** or memorial giving

#### Recurring Donations
- **Sustained support** for long-term programs
- **Automated giving** for convenience
- **Budget planning** with predictable amounts
- **Greater impact** through consistent funding

### Cryptocurrency Options

#### Supported Cryptocurrencies
- **Ethereum (ETH)** - Most widely accepted
- **USDC** - Stable value, good for budgeting
- **USDT** - Another stable option
- **DAI** - Decentralized stablecoin
- **MATIC** - Lower fees on Polygon network

#### Network Selection
- **Ethereum Mainnet**: Higher security, higher fees ($5-50)
- **Polygon**: Lower fees ($0.01-0.10), faster transactions

### Donation Process

#### Step-by-Step Donation
1. **Select organization** from discovery page
2. **Review organization profile** and impact metrics
3. **Choose donation amount** and cryptocurrency
4. **Select network** (Ethereum or Polygon)
5. **Review transaction details** including fees
6. **Confirm in wallet** (MetaMask, etc.)
7. **Wait for confirmation** (1-5 minutes)
8. **Receive receipt** via email and dashboard

#### Smart Contract Automation
- **Recurring donations** via smart contracts
- **Conditional giving** based on milestones
- **Multi-signature donations** for team giving
- **Dollar-cost averaging** for volatile markets

## Security and Best Practices

### Donation Security

#### Before Donating
- **Verify organization credentials**
- **Check wallet address** carefully
- **Confirm network selection**
- **Review transaction details**

#### During Transactions
- **Use reputable wallets** only
- **Double-check recipient** address
- **Set appropriate gas fees**
- **Save transaction hashes**

#### After Donating
- **Confirm transaction** on blockchain
- **Save tax receipts** immediately
- **Update personal records**
- **Monitor for confirmations**

### Avoiding Scams

#### Red Flags
- **Urgent pressure** to donate immediately
- **Requests for private keys** or seed phrases
- **Unverified organizations** asking for funds
- **Too-good-to-be-true** matching offers

#### Verification Steps
- **Check verification badges** on profiles
- **Research organizations** independently
- **Start with small amounts** for testing
- **Report suspicious** activity immediately

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Transaction Problems

#### Failed Donations
- **Check wallet balance** for sufficient funds
- **Verify gas fees** are adequate
- **Confirm network** selection matches wallet
- **Try again** with higher gas price

#### Stuck Transactions
- **Monitor blockchain** explorer for updates
- **Wait for network** congestion to clear
- **Contact support** if delayed over 24 hours
- **Keep transaction hash** for reference

### Account Issues

#### Access Problems
- **Reset password** if forgotten
- **Check email** for account notifications
- **Clear browser cache** and try again
- **Contact support** for persistent issues

#### Wallet Connection
- **Refresh browser** and reconnect
- **Check wallet** is unlocked
- **Update wallet** software if needed
- **Try different browser** or device
