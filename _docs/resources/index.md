## Tools & Calculators

### Financial Tools

#### Donation Impact Calculator
Understand the true impact of your donations:
- **Tax Benefits**: Calculate potential deductions
- **Currency Conversion**: Real-time exchange rates
- **Fee Analysis**: Compare transaction costs
- **Annual Summary**: Year-end giving reports
- **Goal Setting**: Plan your charitable budget

#### Cryptocurrency Converter
Convert between different cryptocurrencies:
- **Live Rates**: Real-time price updates
- **Gas Estimator**: Transaction cost predictions
- **Network Comparison**: Choose the best blockchain
- **Historical Data**: Track price movements
- **Portfolio Value**: Monitor donation values

#### Tax Deduction Estimator
Maximize your tax benefits:
- **Country-Specific**: Localized tax rules
- **Receipt Generator**: Automated documentation
- **Annual Limits**: Track deduction caps
- **Planning Tools**: Optimize giving strategy
- **Export Reports**: Share with tax preparer

#### Giving Strategy Planner
Create a personalized giving plan:
- **Goal Setting**: Define your impact objectives
- **Budget Allocation**: Distribute funds effectively
- **Cause Selection**: Match values to organizations
- **Timeline Planning**: Schedule donations
- **Impact Tracking**: Monitor progress

#### Organization Research Tool
Make informed donation decisions:
- **Verification Status**: Check legitimacy
- **Financial Health**: Review transparency reports
- **Impact Metrics**: Compare effectiveness
- **User Reviews**: Read community feedback
- **Similar Organizations**: Discover alternatives

#### Volunteer Opportunity Matcher
Find the perfect volunteer role:
- **Skills Assessment**: Match your expertise
- **Time Availability**: Filter by commitment
- **Location Options**: Local or remote
- **Cause Alignment**: Support your passions
- **Team Opportunities**: Join group efforts
