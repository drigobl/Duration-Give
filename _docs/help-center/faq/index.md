# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Give Protocol?
Give Protocol is a blockchain-based charitable giving platform that connects donors with organizations and volunteers. We use cryptocurrency technology to ensure transparent, secure, and efficient charitable transactions while providing tax receipts and tracking capabilities.

### How does Give Protocol work?
1. **For Donors**: Create an account, connect your wallet, search for verified organizations, and make donations using cryptocurrency
2. **For Organizations**: Apply for verification, create campaigns, receive donations, and issue tax receipts
3. **For Volunteers**: Offer your time and skills, track hours, and connect with organizations needing help

### Is Give Protocol free to use?
Give Protocol charges minimal platform fees to sustain operations:
- **Platform fee**: 2.5% of donation amount
- **Network fees**: Variable based on blockchain network (usually $1-5)
- No fees for browsing, searching, or creating accounts

## Account & Security

### How do I create an account?
1. Visit [Give Protocol](https://giveprotocol.org)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
3. Choose your account type (Donor, Organization, or Volunteer)
4. Complete the registration form
5. Verify your email address
6. Connect your cryptocurrency wallet

### What wallets are supported?
We support major Web3 wallets including:
- MetaMask
- WalletConnect
- Coinbase Wallet
- Rainbow Wallet
- Trust Wallet

### How is my data protected?
- All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- We never store private keys or wallet passwords
- Personal information is protected under our Privacy Policy
- Smart contracts are audited for security
- Two-factor authentication available for all accounts

## Donations

### What cryptocurrencies can I donate?
Currently supported cryptocurrencies:
- Ethereum (ETH)
- USDC
- USDT
- DAI
- MATIC (on Polygon network)

### Can I get a tax receipt for my donation?
Yes! Verified organizations can issue official tax receipts for your donations. Tax receipts are:
- Generated automatically after donation confirmation
- Sent to your registered email
- Available in your account dashboard
- Valid for tax deduction purposes (consult your tax advisor)

### What happens if I send to the wrong address?
Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible. Always double-check:
- The organization's wallet address
- The network you're using
- The amount before confirming
Contact support immediately if you make an error - we'll try to help recover funds if possible.

### Can I make recurring donations?
Yes! Set up recurring donations by:
1. Finding your chosen organization
2. Clicking "Set up recurring donation"
3. Choosing frequency (weekly, monthly, yearly)
4. Setting the amount
5. Authorizing automated transactions

## Organizations

### How do organizations get verified?
Organizations must:
1. Submit proof of charitable status
2. Provide government registration documents
3. Complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification
4. Pass our review process (typically 5-7 business days)
5. Maintain good standing with regular audits

### Can international organizations join?
Yes! We welcome organizations worldwide. Requirements vary by country, but generally include:
- Proof of charitable/non-profit status
- Government registration
- Bank account for fiat conversion (optional)
- Compliance with local regulations

### How do organizations receive funds?
Organizations can:
- Keep funds in cryptocurrency
- Convert to fiat currency through our partners
- Set up automatic conversions
- Access funds through their dashboard
- Withdraw to bank accounts (where available)

## Volunteering

### How does volunteer time tracking work?
1. Volunteers log hours through the platform
2. Organizations verify completed hours
3. Time is recorded on the blockchain
4. Volunteers receive verification badges
5. Build a verifiable volunteer resume

### Can I volunteer remotely?
Yes! Many organizations offer remote volunteering opportunities including:
- Digital marketing
- Content creation
- Translation services
- Technical support
- Virtual tutoring

## Technical Questions

### What blockchain does Give Protocol use?
We primarily use Ethereum and Polygon networks for:
- Lower transaction fees on Polygon
- Higher security on Ethereum mainnet
- Cross-chain compatibility
- Future expansion to other networks

### Are smart contracts audited?
Yes, all smart contracts are:
- Audited by reputable third-party firms
- Open-source and verifiable
- Regularly updated for security
- Transparent on blockchain explorers

### What about gas fees?
- Polygon network: Usually under $0.01
- Ethereum mainnet: Varies ($5-50 depending on network congestion)
- We show estimated fees before confirmation
- Consider using Polygon for smaller donations

## Troubleshooting

### My donation isn't showing up
1. Check the transaction hash on a blockchain explorer
2. Ensure you used the correct network
3. Wait for network confirmations (2-30 minutes)
4. Contact support with transaction details

### I can't connect my wallet
Try these steps:
1. Update your wallet to the latest version
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Try a different browser
5. Ensure you're on the correct network

### The site isn't loading properly
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page (Ctrl/Cmd + R)
- Clear browser cache
- Disable ad blockers for our domain
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
