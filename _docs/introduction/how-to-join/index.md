### Create Your Account

#### Sign Up Process
1. **Visit Give Protocol**
   - Go to [giveprotocol.org](https://giveprotocol.org)
   - Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
   - Choose your account type

2. **Choose Account Type**
   - **Individual**: Personal volunteer and donor account
   - **Organization**: Nonprofit organization account
   - **Corporate**: Business account for employee engagement
   - **Educational**: School or university account

3. **Provide Basic Information**
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Password (secure and unique)
   - Location (city, state/province, country)

4. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the link to confirm your account
   - Return to Give Protocol to complete setup

### Account Setup Options

#### Quick Setup (5 minutes)
- Basic profile information
- Primary interests
- Location preferences
- Start browsing immediately

#### Complete Setup (15 minutes)
- Detailed profile completion
- Skills and experience
- Availability preferences
- Photo upload
- Background check initiation (if volunteering)

### Identity Verification

#### Why Verification Matters
- Builds trust with organizations
- Ensures safety for all participants
- Enables access to more opportunities
- Protects against fraud and abuse

#### Verification Process
1. **Email Verification**: Confirm your email address
2. **Phone Verification**: Text message confirmation (optional)
3. **Photo ID**: Upload government-issued identification
4. **Address Confirmation**: Utility bill or bank statement
5. **Social Media**: Link professional profiles (optional)

### Background Checks

#### When Required
- Working with vulnerable populations
- Handling money or valuables
- Access to sensitive information
- Leadership positions
- High-responsibility roles

#### Types of Checks
- **Criminal Background**: Local, state, and federal records
- **Reference Checks**: Professional and personal references
- **Education Verification**: Degree and certification confirmation
- **Employment History**: Work experience verification
- **Specialized Clearances**: Sector-specific requirements

#### Process Timeline
- **Initiation**: Start check after profile completion
- **Processing**: 3-10 business days typically
- **Results**: Notification via email and platform
- **Appeals**: Process for disputed results
- **Renewal**: Periodic updates as required

### Security Settings

#### Password Security
- **Strong Passwords**: 12+ characters with mixed case, numbers, symbols
- **Unique Passwords**: Don't reuse from other accounts
- **Password Manager**: Consider using password management tools
- **Regular Updates**: Change passwords periodically

#### Two-Factor Authentication
1. **Enable 2FA**: Go to Security Settings
2. **Choose Method**: SMS, authenticator app, or hardware key
3. **Backup Codes**: Save recovery codes securely
4. **Test Setup**: Verify functionality

#### Privacy Controls
- **Profile Visibility**: Choose who can see your information
- **Contact Information**: Control how organizations reach you
- **Activity Sharing**: Manage social media integration
- **Data Settings**: Configure information sharing preferences

### Safety Guidelines

#### Personal Safety
- **Meet in Public**: Initial meetings in public places
- **Trust Your Instincts**: If something feels wrong, don't proceed
- **Verify Organizations**: Check credentials and reviews
- **Share Plans**: Tell someone about your volunteer activities
- **Emergency Contacts**: Keep contact information readily available

#### Information Security
- **Protect Personal Data**: Don't overshare sensitive information
- **Secure Communications**: Use platform messaging for coordination
- **Document Safety**: Keep records of all volunteer agreements
- **Report Issues**: Use platform reporting tools for concerns

### Account Setup Problems

#### Email Verification Issues
- **Check Spam Folder**: Verification emails sometimes filtered
- **Whitelist Give Protocol**: Add to trusted senders
- **Try Different Email**: Use alternative email address
- **Contact Support**: Get manual verification assistance
- **Browser Issues**: Try different browser or clear cache

#### Profile Completion Difficulties
- **Required Fields**: Ensure all mandatory information provided
- **Photo Upload**: Check file size and format requirements
- **Skills Selection**: Choose from provided options
- **Location Services**: Enable GPS for accurate matching
- **Browser Compatibility**: Use supported browsers

### Getting Started Challenges

- **Response Time**: Organizations may take 24-48 hours
- **Contact Methods**: Use platform messaging system
