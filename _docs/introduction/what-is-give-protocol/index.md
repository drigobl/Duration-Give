<div class="content-section">
  <h2>Overview</h2>
  <p>Give Protocol is a next-generation charitable giving platform that leverages blockchain technology to create a transparent, efficient, and globally accessible ecosystem for donations and volunteering. By combining the power of cryptocurrency with traditional charitable giving, we're building a future where every donation is traceable, every organization is accountable, and every act of kindness is recorded immutably.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-section">
  <h2>Our Mission</h2>
  <p>To revolutionize charitable giving by:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Eliminating barriers</strong> between donors and causes they care about</li>
    <li><strong>Ensuring transparency</strong> in how donations are used</li>
    <li><strong>Reducing costs</strong> associated with traditional donation processing</li>
    <li><strong>Empowering organizations</strong> with modern fundraising tools</li>
    <li><strong>Creating trust</strong> through blockchain verification</li>
  </ul>
</div>

    <span>Why Blockchain?</span>
    <svg class="section-icon" width="24" height="24" viewBox="0 0 20 20" fill="none">

    <h3>Transparency</h3>
    <p>Every transaction is recorded on a public ledger, ensuring complete visibility into how funds are used.</p>

    <h3>Security</h3>
    <p>Cryptographic security ensures donations reach intended recipients without intermediary risks.</p>

    <h3>Efficiency</h3>
    <p>Direct transfers eliminate multiple intermediaries, reducing costs and delays.</p>

    <h3>Trust</h3>
    <p>Immutable records create an auditable trail that builds confidence in the donation process.</p>

    <h3>Innovation</h3>
    <p>Smart contracts enable automated processes like recurring donations and conditional giving.</p>
