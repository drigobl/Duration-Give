## Donation Features

### Flexible Giving Options
- **One-Time Donations**: Support causes instantly
- **Recurring Donations**: Set up automatic monthly contributions
- **Campaign Support**: Contribute to specific fundraising goals
- **Emergency Response**: Rapid deployment for crisis situations
- **Matching Programs**: Double your impact with corporate matches

### Cryptocurrency Support
- **Multiple Currencies**: Accept major cryptocurrencies
- **Stablecoin Options**: Minimize volatility with USDC, USDT
- **Real-Time Conversion**: Current exchange rates displayed
- **Low Fees**: Minimal transaction costs
- **Cross-Chain Support**: Donate from different blockchains

### Impact Tracking
- **Real-Time Updates**: See your donation in action
- **Progress Reports**: Regular updates from organizations
- **Impact Metrics**: Quantifiable results of your contribution
- **Beneficiary Stories**: Direct feedback from those helped
- **Annual Summaries**: Comprehensive yearly impact reports
