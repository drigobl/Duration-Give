## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- **A modern web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- **A cryptocurrency wallet** (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or similar)
- **Some cryptocurrency** for donations or gas fees
- **Basic understanding** of blockchain transactions (we'll help with this!)

## Platform Overview

### Core Features

- **Transparent Donations**: Every transaction is recorded on the blockchain
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Donate using various cryptocurrencies
- **Impact Tracking**: See exactly how your contributions make a difference
- **Verification System**: Trust verified organizations and volunteers
- **Community Features**: Connect with like-minded individuals

### How It Works

1. **Create Account**: Sign up and complete your profile
2. **Connect Wallet**: Link your crypto wallet securely
3. **Explore**: Browse organizations, causes, or volunteer opportunities
4. **Take Action**: Donate, volunteer, or list your organization
5. **Track Impact**: Monitor your contributions and their effects
